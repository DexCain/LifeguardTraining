Welcome to Lifeguard Training!

About the Creator:

Hello, my name is Declan Cain. I have 4 years of experience in aquatics, two of which were spent in a management and training role. Thus needlessly to say aquatic saftey is something that is very important to me, and something that I have built substaintal knowledge in. Additonally, I am an aspiring software developer. This passion project is thus an intersection of both of these two interests of mine.

About the Lifesaving Project:

I created this game as lifesaving and life support can be hard to remember your first, second, and even twentieth time training. Through this little game that I created, I hope you guys can have fun and become better lifeguards. While this isn't a substiution for real hands-on training, it can be a fun and gameified addition to the otherwise mundane training.

Final Note:

Again, thank you for playing this game, and hopefully you learn or remember a thing or two!
